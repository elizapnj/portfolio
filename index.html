Travel recommendations

Chamonix, France

The view of Mont Blanc covered in snow, is a once in a lifetime experience.

Lisbon, Portugal

If you feel brave enough (and have sneakers!), I encourage you to explore Lisbon on foot!

You will find great detail in all external building decorations.

Milano, Italy

Milano has an elegant and glamorous atmosphere.

Try any ice cream covered on top with hot chocolate.

Annecy, France

You can stroll along the canals by feeding the ducks and the swans

or even spend a sunny day by the lake and eat your lunch at the Restaurant Ship.

Sintra, Portugal

It will feel as if you walked in a scene of Game of Thrones.

Watch out for the dragons!

Munich, Germany

You can visit Munich within a day.

A great place for beer lovers!

Dhermi, Albania

You will have many options to enjoy the crystal clear waters, such as paragliding and jet skiing.

A must visit

Bologna, Italy

Gnocchi, gnocchi, gnocchi, gnocchi...

Need I say more? Just go there and order it, you can thank me later.

Jale, Albania

A natural beauty.

Great for camping.

Bansko, Bulgaria

A value for money choice, for anyone who loves snow.

Loutraki, Greece

An hour away from Athens, lies a hidden gem, Hraion.

A unique experience where you can have a swim next to the ancient temple of Hera.
